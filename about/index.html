Hi! I'm Victor Lai, a Software Engineering Undergraduate studying at the University of Waterloo. It is my second year of programming, but I am quick to learn and eager to try, well, just about anything, and always to expand my horizons. I love solving problems, programming or otherwise, and learning about all sorts of technologies, whether it involves my personalized computer, my audiophile audio system, or as they show up on HackerNews.

I am passionate about simple, functional and aesthetically pleasing design, and program with the belief that if I make something I enjoy using, I am sure someone else will enjoy it as well.
