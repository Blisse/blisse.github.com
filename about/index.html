My alias is Bliss \'blis\, which is not only my favourite word, but also one of my favourite songs.

I am passionate, stubborn, extremely hard working, flexible, determined and intense. That's the good. The bad? I'm not as confident in my abilities as I want to be. I'm still growing and learning. I make mistakes. I sometimes get emotional and lose my cool.

Technology-wise, I'm quick to learn and eager to prove myself. And now I'm going to go after my goals with a passion. I love all sorts of technology and I try to keep up to date. And I love learning about anything technology related. In particular, I love design and logic.

So? I won't let anything stop me from getting what I want. Not even myself.

I use this site to blog about all my random shenanigans — random lessons, random musings, general thoughts. And to keep track of all the cool things I've found and used.
