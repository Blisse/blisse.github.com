Hi, I'm a 19 year old Software Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. I am passionate about learning, challenging myself, and I'm not afraid of pushing my limits.

I write about anything I'm interested in, if only to keep up my writing skills. Also it's nice to see how I've grown over time.

I was first exposed to programming in Grade 10. I really enjoyed programming in Grade 11, but never knew that was where I would end up, so I went into Grade 12 not knowing where to go after I graduated. When I was self-learning programming for Waterloo's Canadian Computing Contest, something clicked for me with programming, and I went to Waterloo, not knowing exactly why. After an interview in my first year, something just clicked in my mind. That interview was in March 2012.

I understand now what programming means to me, and why it clicked. And I love it.

I enjoy simple, functional and aesthetically pleasing designs, I program and I create things with the belief that if I make something I like using, I am sure someone else will like it as well.

When I'm not coding, I am gaming, learning the guitar, practicing Kendo or Badminton, going for a run or listening to music.

My alias is Bliss \'blis\, which is not only my favourite word, but also one of my favourite songs.
