Who am I

I'm Victor Lai, a 19 year old software developer sometimes in Toronto, Canada and studying at the University of Waterloo.

I love music, technology, working my ass off, getting stuff done, and breaking my body. Oh, and of course, programming.

When I get going, I am passionate, stubborn, hard-working, determined and intense. I'm not as confident in my abilities as I want to be, but I'm still growing and learning. I make mistakes. I sometimes lose my cool. But I never give up.

Technology-wise, I particularly love design and logic. I'm still learning, but I prefer the strength of Python.

Nothing can stop me from getting what I want to be. Not even myself.

I use this site to blog about all my random shenanigans — random lessons, random musings, general thoughts. And to keep track of all the cool things I've found and used.
