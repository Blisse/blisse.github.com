Hi! My name is Victor Lai. I'm a Software Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. I'm passionate about UI/UX, design, gaming and interactions.

I love learning, and love learning from my mistakes. It's also an amazing feeling to take a step back and look at your progress, then dive right back in with the new perspective, and renewed enthusiasm – try it now, use your mouse with your non-dominant hand.

I find it cool to see my experiences warped by the world around me and see my views change as I experience more. When I'm not programming or studying, I'm out having fun. This year, I'm learning how to skate so hopefully I can play hockey. In my spare time, I blog, run, read, play video games, and explore.
