About Me

My alias is Bliss \'blis\, which is not only my favourite word, but also one of my favourite songs.

who

I'm Victor Lai, a 19 year old software developer sometimes in Toronto, Canada but usually at the University of Waterloo. I love music, technology, cat pictures, writing, reading, meditating, and running.

I aim to be the most intense that I can be at anything I do, while keeping the part of me that keeps me relaxed. And I love pushing my limits every single time.

me

In particular, I love playing badminton. I recently started some kendo and love it. While I've always swam and ran, I'm learning my way around the gym now.

I'm a huge supporter of Windows, despite it losing its complete dominance, and despite being a programmer (apparently it's a sin?). My favourite language by far is still Python, though I'm stuck in the space between 2.7 and 3.x. Of course, English comes a close second.

Everything interests me. Sometimes it gets distracting and I have to purposely zone out things. But I enjoy having the flexibility of actually making that decision.

I use this site to preserve my life — lessons that change me, random musings about myself, some other stuff I want to change people with. And to keep track of some cool things.
