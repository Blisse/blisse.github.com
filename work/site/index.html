My Website

I've been doing a lot of work on my website for the past year, but I managed to keep track of how it looks. Here's a nice image gallery of the progression of my site's theme.

If you want to take a look at the source code, you can find it on Github. It is generated through Jekyll whenever I push to Github.

Motivation

It's been nearly 2 years since I first built my site, and I've been really limited by my decision to not use Javascript. Well. no more. While my site isn't that perfect measure of "responsive-ness" anymore, and pretty broken on mobile devices, I finally get to create a design that I really enjoy looking at. A very simple, laidback style that fits what I've been trying to achieve, but couldn't because of how I limited myself to only using CSS. Well. this is the result, and it's the culmination of so much of my design knowledge.
