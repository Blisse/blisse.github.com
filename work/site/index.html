My Website

I've been doing a lot of work on my website for the past year, but I managed to keep track of how it looks. Here's a nice image gallery of the progression of my site's theme.

If you want to take a look at the source code, you can find it on Github. It is generated through Jekyll whenever I push to Github.

Right now, my goal with this site is to make it squeeky clean. Rather than using block elements to section out everything, I'm going to depend on strong typography with a light dose of blocks and icons in order to section my site out. It looks very plain and clean, but I enjoy this theme more than the ones in the past. It doesn't have the sting and really unique look of my site by November 2012, but I like it regardless.
