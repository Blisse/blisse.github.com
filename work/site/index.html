Motivation

Everytime I revamp this I have to change this description. I should've really kept a better changelog.

I think this meets my original goal way better. I've moved on to the more powerful Foundation framework for my backend, which has allowed me to simplify a lot of my structures and in return lets me keep the minimalistic, soft look that I wanted to achieve from the beginning. Who knew the right framework would have such an effect!?

Simple, elegant, mobile, and easy to use.
